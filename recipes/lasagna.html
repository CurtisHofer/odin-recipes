Worlds Best Lasagna!

Description

This is the legendary lasagna recipe from the legend himself John Chandler. Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

Layer the following in order twice

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Cook for 50 minutes at 375 degrees F. Cook with covered foil for the first 25 minutes, then uncovered for the rest
